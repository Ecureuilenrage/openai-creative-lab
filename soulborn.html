Pivots

Pivots

A water-filled world with seven suns was visually difficult because those combinations barely exist in the references seen by the models. The turning point came from a productive decision: adding an animated object or presence — the shark, the creature — made the scene clearer and more alive. What started as a constraint became a narrative strength, giving each world a living anchor that guides the viewer through the journey.
