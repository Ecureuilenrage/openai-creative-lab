Voici 10 directions musicales différentes, du plus atmosphérique au plus épique.

Puis J'ai choisi celle là : 4— Traversée des mondes (transitions rapides)

1 — Le souffle du monolithe (ambiance pure, intro)

Dark ambient drone, deep sub-bass rumble, distant metallic resonance, single sustained cello note, crystalline digital arpeggios fading in and out, vast emptiness, sci-fi, no drums, cinematic, 60 BPM

2 — Le voyageur (thème principal mélancolique)

Melancholic orchestral theme, solo duduk melody over sparse strings, slow build, desert wind atmosphere, lonely wanderer, ancient and futuristic, cinematic trailer, emotional, 70 BPM

3 — Sept soleils (montée épique)

Epic cinematic orchestral, massive brass crescendo, layered choir voices chanting in Latin, thundering taiko drums, rising tension, fantasy RPG trailer, Hans Zimmer style, powerful, 90 BPM

4 — Traversée des mondes (transitions rapides)

Hybrid orchestral electronic, glitchy synth textures layered with full orchestra, rapid percussive hits, whooshes between sections, each section  shifts genre slightly, cinematic trailer music, intense, 110 BPM

5 — La terre brûlée (sombre, post-apo)

Dark industrial cinematic, distorted bass drops, grinding metallic textures, sparse war drums, ominous low brass, post-apocalyptic atmosphere, oppressive, Blade Runner meets Dark Souls, 80 BPM

6 — Le jardin d'Éden (renaissance, beauté)

Ethereal ambient orchestral, female wordless vocals floating over lush strings, gentle harp arpeggios, soft piano, nature sounds woven in, hopeful, emotional, rebirth, cinematic, 65 BPM

7 — SOLBORNE main theme (thème signature AAA)

AAA video game main theme, heroic orchestral with electronic hybrid elements, memorable 4-note motif repeated by French horn then full orchestra, building from quiet piano to massive cinematic climax, epic drums, choir, 100 BPM

8 — Bande-annonce cuts (trailer rhythm, pour le montage)

Cinematic trailer music, dramatic pauses and hits, bass drops on each cut, escalating tension, brass stabs, snare rolls building to silence then massive impact, designed for quick-cut editing, hybrid orchestral, 95 BPM

9 — Les glaces éternelles (arctique, isolation)

Arctic ambient, frozen textures, crackling ice sound design, high-pitched glass-like synths, slow cello solo, vast reverb, lonely, cold, beautiful desolation, Interstellar meets Journey game soundtrack, 55 BPM

10 — Title card reveal (le hit final 10 secondes)

Single massive cinematic impact hit followed by deep reverberating sub-bass decay, crystalline high-frequency shimmer rising, one heartbeat thump, then silence, sound design, trailer logo reveal, no melody, pure power, 5 seconds of epic
